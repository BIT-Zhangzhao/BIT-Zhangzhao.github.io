I received the B.Eng. degree with honors in Automation from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT), Beijing, China, in 2021. I once studied Electrical & Computer Engineering in University of Washington (UW), Seattle, USA, in 2019. I am currently pursing the Ph.D. degree with the National Key Lab of Autonomous Intelligent Unmanned Systems, BIT, Beijing, China.

My current research interests include operations research and machine learning for solving complex real-world systems under uncertainty. I have authored 6 high-quality Research Papers in the field of operations research, optimization, machine learning, and their applications in facility location, routing problems, spatial analysis and optimization. I have applied or been issued 8 C.N. Patents. I participate in research projects exceeds ¥ 30,000,000 supported by National Natural Science Foundations of China (NSFC), National Key Research and Development Program of China, etc.
